If you're an owner or administrator in your workspace, you can restrict who in your Workspace has access to your agent.

<Callout variant="info">Access control requires a [Team plan](https://botpress.com/pricing) or higher.</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Go to **
    <Icon icon="Settings" /> Settings** in your agent's menu.
  </Step>
  <Step>Select **Access Control**.</Step>
</Steps>

Here, you can select which members of your Workspace have access to your agent:

<Frame>
  <img alt="Access control" class="block dark:hidden" src="/assets/manage-your-agent/access-control.png" />
  <img alt="Access control" class="hidden dark:block" src="/assets/manage-your-agent/access-control-dark.png" />
</Frame>

When you're done, select **Save**.

## Access control vs. roles

Access control is an _agent-level setting_—it doesn't change the workspace member's access to any other agents or their overall role in the workspace. You can configure workspace-level roles in your [Workspace settings](/get-started/configure-your-workspace#manage-roles).

<Callout variant="note">Workspace owners and administrators always have access to all bots in a workspace.</Callout>
